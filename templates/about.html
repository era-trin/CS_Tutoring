Our Story

Founded in the heart of Harlem, we are a student-led initiative driven by a shared passion for coding and community. After experiencing the transformative power of Computer Science education at Trinity, we recognized a pressing need: to bridge the gap between opportunity and access for the next generation. Our mission is simple yet powerful — to empower students with the skills and confidence to thrive in a digital world. Through hands-on coding education, mentorship, and real-world problem solving, we’re equipping young minds with the tools of the future. We believe that code isn’t just about syntax — it’s about unlocking creativity, building resilience, and opening doors. And we’re here to make sure those doors are wide open for every student we reach.
