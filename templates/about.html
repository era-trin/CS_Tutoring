Our Story

As we’ve grown, we’ve seen firsthand how the tech industry is expanding at an unprecedented pace and how it’s poised to shape the future of every field.

Yet, without action, this growth will deepen existing wealth disparities, leaving underrepresented communities behind.

We believe that access to high-quality coding education is the key to ensuring that the next generation of engineers is diverse, equitable, and empowered to thrive.

Our work is driven by the conviction that talent is universal, but opportunity is not, and we’re here to change that.
